Dong Viet Hoang

Hoang V. Dong · Researcher in recommender systems and LLMs. Builder of small, useful things.

I'm a final-year PhD candidate in Computer Science at SMU School of Computing and Information Systems, advised by Assoc. Prof. Yuan Fang and Assoc. Prof. Hady W. Lauw. My research looks at how we can make recommendation and retrieval systems more faithful, fair, and aware of user intent — most recently through contrastive and LLM-assisted methods. Outside the lab I run two ventures: Mavenpath, a live e-learning platform, and Hai Trinh Tau Thuyen, an offline-first system for digitising Vietnam's fishing logbooks.

Contrastive Alignment for Review-aware Recs
